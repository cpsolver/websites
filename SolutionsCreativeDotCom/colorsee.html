ColorSee TM Filter: Allows Colorblind to Distinguish Colors

Instructions: Print on a transparency sheet using a color printer, cut out one of the circular images, put the ColorSee TM filter in front of a colored area, and compare the brightness in each of the three areas. The brighter the image in the B area, the more Blue the area contains. The brighter the image in the R area, the more Red the area contains. The brighter the image in the G area, the more Green the area contains.
